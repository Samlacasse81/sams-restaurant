The beach restaurants history

The beach restaurant was an historique building called the train wistle, during the 2end world war it was a little diner on Florida key island right on the water front, now its a high class restaurant, newly updated and modern. We added a kitchen, a balcony outside for more setting and we also have some tables and chairs on the grasse even more setting, since its a tiny bulding. We also offer tours on Sundays because the restaurant is closed, from hours 1-2pm and 3-4pm. Its 20$ per person and that includes drinks.
